About Me

I am a research and insights professional, experienced in conducting primary research and utilizing syndicated research and industry data sources to solve critical business questions. I have experience leading thought leadership consumer research in Financial Services and have also used a variety of research methodologies in Consumer-Packaged Goods (CPG) and Telecommunications & Technology verticals.

As a consumer researcher, I am naturally curious about understanding people, their evolving lifestyles and habits, and what implications these shifts have. My research efforts have leveraged a wide range of quantitative (e.g., surveys, data analytics) and qualitative (e.g., IDIs, online communities) techniques. At the heart of these efforts, I delight in helping businesses and organizations better align with client needs and motivations. I also had the opportunity to apply research and analytics expertise in areas such as advertising (e.g., copy testing, campaign performance), branding (perception), product (satisfaction, feature evaluations), competitive benchmarking, and segmentation (e.g., life stage, behavioral, and attitudinal).

Over the course of my career, I developed a strong interest in combining insights with data analytics. Last fall, I pursued Caltech CTME's Data Science program for professionals as an incremental step. It has been a rewarding experience - not only in equipping me with data analytics/science tools but also in revealing new ways of approaching problems and developing solutions. I look forward to applying my research consulting, analytical, and problem-solving expertise in the next chapter of my career.

Projects

Customer Analytics & Insights

Analyzing the patterns of historical sales data offers valuable insights and strategic advantages, enabling businesses to understand their customers on a deeper level.

Through cohort analysis, companies can uncover long-term trends in customer behavior, while RFM analysis segments customers based on recency, frequency, and monetary value. Additionally, cluster analysis helps identify various customer groups, such as high-value segments. These actionable insights can drive decision-making, allow companies to personalize marketing, optimize customer experiences, and foster customer loyalty.

Data Visualization (Tableau)

Data visualization plays a crucial role in simplifying complex volumes of data, making it easily understandable and accessible.
